‚ö†Ô∏è Signs You Need Help Right Now

Please reach out immediately if you're experiencing:

  • Thoughts of hurting yourself or others
  • Feeling hopeless or trapped with no way out
  • Severe panic or overwhelming anxiety
  • Hearing voices or seeing things others don't
  • Intense mood swings affecting your safety
  • Unable to take care of basic needs

üõ°Ô∏è Right Now: Keep Yourself Safe

1. Remove any means of harm If you're having thoughts of self-harm, move away from anything that could hurt you. Go to a safe space.
2. Call a crisis helpline immediately Dial 9152987821 or 1860-2662-345. A caring person is ready to help you right now.
3. Tell someone you trust Reach out to a family member, friend, teacher, or counselor. You don't have to face this alone.
4. If alone, stay in a public place Being around others can help you feel safer until you can get professional help.
5. Use grounding techniques Name 5 things you see, 4 things you can touch, 3 things you hear, 2 things you smell, 1 thing you taste. This can help during panic.
