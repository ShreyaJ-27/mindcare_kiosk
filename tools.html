Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Release physical tension by tensing and relaxing different muscle groups.

Step 1: Start with your toes. Curl them tightly for 5 seconds, then release.
Step 2: Move to your calves. Tense for 5 seconds, then release.
Step 3: Continue up your body: thighs, stomach, hands, arms, shoulders, face.
Step 4: Notice the difference between tension and relaxation. Your body will feel looser and calmer.

üí° Do this lying down or sitting comfortably. It takes about 10-15 minutes.

Safe Place Visualization

Create a calm mental space you can visit anytime.

Close your eyes (or keep them open if more comfortable)
Imagine a place where you feel completely safe and peaceful. It could be real or imaginary - a beach, forest, cozy room, anywhere you feel calm.
Notice the details: What do you see? What sounds do you hear? What can you smell? How does it feel?
Stay there for a few minutes. Breathe slowly. When you're ready, gently open your eyes.

üí° You can return to this safe place whenever you feel stressed or overwhelmed.
